WHO WE ARE

Martinville Acoustics, a renowned company in the cinema sector has executed numerous cinemas in MENA region. We are Acoustic consultants providing turnkey construction solutions. With employees around the globe, we have designed and executed numerous turnkey projects around the region. We look forward to supporting the construction, acoustics, and design needs of the entertainment sector in the region.

The Organization has grown continually over the years by taking a professional approach focusing on developing and providing only the highest quality of systems and services to clients. We are currently undertaking turnkey projects with in-house capacities for specialized jobs in the field of Acoustic Engineering, Sound Engineering, Cinemas, Civil/Structural, Engineering and Electromechanical engineering. We are capable of providing Design, Build solutions to the construction and Entertainment industries in Middle East, Africa and Asia.

We deliver high end technology, quality design, engineering, procurement, construction and project management services at good value and continually strengthen our solid reputation by providing practical and innovative technology and solutions to our valued clients in a most efficient and professional fashion. The organization thrives with offices across UAE, Bahrain, Oman, KSA, India, and Africa.

The scope of work involves specializing in survey, concept, development, planning, scheduling and execution of projects. A complete tailor made solution is provided for specific needs. We work with efficient and cost effective methods to provide the best in class services to our clients.
