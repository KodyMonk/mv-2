WHAT WE DO

Cinemas

We are a leading home cinema and commercial cinema installation specialists. Our designs and installations are second-to-none, as we use high-performance cinema systems, powerful cinema speakers and extremely good music systems. We are expert cinema installers with a passion for perfect systems that you can use every day.

ACOUSTICS & SOUNDPROOFING

We offer a wide range of acoustic solutions, ranging from domestic soundproofing, commercial soundproofing to industrial soundproofing projects. Our acoustic solutions and services can be installed efficiently to reduce airborne sound pollution. Contact us to see how we can help solve the sound problem.

INTERIORS & FITOUTS

At Martinville Acoustics, we provide a superb interior fit -out service with quality delivery, sustainability and safety in mind. We undertake works consisting of re-modeling and modernization of existing buildings together with fitting out new build shell and core for building owners, landlords, tenants and end users.
